A Taste of Music

Design Project

A taste of music is a conceptual album cover series explorating how sound can translate to visual design. The collection spans eight distinct genres, each cover reflecting the unique mood and characteristics.

Album collection cover

Baggy Jeans - NCT U

Genre: Kpop

A bold, high-energy cover for the experimental, playful nature of the song.

Torpedoes - KMFDM

Genre: Industrial rock

The design express the songs antiwar messaging whilst sticking true to industrial rock's raw, mechanical aesthetic.

Blue Suede Shoes - Carl Perkins

Genre: Rock'n Roll

Inspired by early rock and roll's culture, the design uses bold typography and a vintage inspired color palette to capture genres energy.

Collard Greens -
SchoolBoy Q ft. Kendrick Lamar

Genre: Hip Hop

Strong contrasts and graphical elements convey confidence, reflecting hip hop's bold visuals and the strong energy of the song.
