Sjöstugan

Web Design

The concept for Sjöstugan is a family owned Bed & Breakfast where the guests stay in rustic cabins located on the property.

Role:

UX & UI design

Scope:

Research, IA, flows, visual design, prototyping

Platform:

Custom front-end build (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)

Final desktop homepage

Problem

Users needed a way to quickly find all the relevant information, understand offerings, and reach out to the owners regarding interest.

The business needed a webpage that conveyed the spirit of the brand and instilled trust in their audience...

Key user needs & flows

To ensure key tasks were frictionless, I mapped the primary user needs to clear navigation and content flows.

Sjöstugans color palette is highly inspired by the traditional colors of the Dalecarlia region, adapted to be suitable for the web.

The falu-red is the signature color for houses in the area, as well as the colors of the cabins that the guests stay in.

The decorative illustrations draw from the traditional pattern called kurbits, simplified slightly for the use case.

The typography balances an expressive script for brand tone with elegant headings and a highly readable serif for bodytext.

Homepage scroll interaction

Project Outcome

The final website conveys the feeling of the brand, whilst featuring all the necessary information.

The page is responsive working for the guest booking leisurely on their computer as well as the last minute booker on their phone.

Future implementations:

The homepage explore section could be customised based on the season to attract more visitors.
