Sunshake

Brand Identity

Sunshake is a conceptual brand identity for a juice and smoothie bar.

The goal was to create an energetic identity that is scalable across both physical and digital applications.

Deliverables:

Logo design · color and typography systems · patterns · packaging · posters · environmental applications.

Concept

Sunshakes identity is inspired by natural energy and warmth. The visuals take from the sun and natural ingredients.

The identity uses bold expressive shapes and a balanced colorpalette to feel friendly and inviting rather than premium and exclusive.
